Collections of photos scraped from Google Street View in each of New York City’s five boroughs. All photos have been run through OCR so that the text in them is searchable. A search for a specific street is likely to turn up storefront signs and delivery vans. A search for the word “best" shows pizza parlors that claim to sell the best pizza in town and Best Buy stores.

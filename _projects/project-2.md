All 'one way' signs appearances in NYC from Google Street View.

A comprehensive collection of "one way" sign appearances captured from Google Street View throughout New York City, from 2007 to 2024. The dataset contains 734,700 signs. While the website showcases the imagery, the dataset is also accessible through a unique redemption system using special scratch cards.

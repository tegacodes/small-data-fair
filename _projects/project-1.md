An image dataset of all of Mark Zuckerberg's hairstyles.

The artist describes his process:

"I discovered an open source hair detector and have used it on hundreds of images of Mark Zuckerberg to build what I believe is the most comprehensive archive of Zuckerberg haircuts in existence. Thank you AI researchers!"
